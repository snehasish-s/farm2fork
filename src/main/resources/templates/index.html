Our Process

Simple steps from farm to fork

Farmer Creates Batch

The farmer registers a new batch of produce on the blockchain with details like origin, harvest date, and initial quality checks.

Distributor Updates Transfer

Distributors scan and update the batch information as it moves through the supply chain, adding storage conditions and transit details.

Retailer Receives Batch

Retailers verify the batch upon arrival and update the blockchain with shelf life information and storage conditions.

Consumer Checks History

Consumers scan QR codes to view the complete history of the product, verifying its origin and journey.
