About Farm2Fork

Transforming the agricultural supply chain with blockchain technology

Our Story

Farm2Fork was born from a simple idea: creating transparency in our food supply chain.

Founded in 2020, our platform connects farmers directly with consumers, ensuring that every step of the journey from farm to fork is recorded on an immutable blockchain. This creates trust, improves food safety, and supports sustainable farming practices.

We believe that everyone has the right to know where their food comes from, how it was grown, and the path it took to reach their plate. Our technology makes this possible for the first time at scale.

Our Mission

To create a transparent, secure, and efficient food supply chain that benefits farmers, distributors, retailers, and consumers alike through blockchain technology.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for food traceability, ensuring that by 2030, everyone can trace the origin of their food with a simple scan.
